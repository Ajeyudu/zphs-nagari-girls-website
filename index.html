About the School

ZPHS Nagari (Girls) is a girls-only government school located in the Nagari block of Chittoor district, Andhra Pradesh. Established in 1962 and managed by the local body, the school has been a cornerstone of girls' education in the region for decades.

The school offers classes from Grade 6 to Grade 10 and follows English as the medium of instruction. It is situated in an urban area and does not include a pre-primary section. Despite infrastructure challenges, the school is easily accessible by all-weather roads, and its academic session begins every April.

Total Students: 565
Teachers: 28
