ZPHS Nagari (Girls)

About School

ZPHS NAGARI (GIRLS) is a girls-only government school located in the Nagari block of Chittoor district, Andhra Pradesh. Established in 1962 and managed by the local body, the school has been a cornerstone of girls' education in the region for decades. The school offers classes from Grade 6 to Grade 10 and follows English as the medium of instruction. Total Students: 565 | Teachers: 28 | Classrooms: 17
